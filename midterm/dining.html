Places to Eat

Planning your next meal for your trip to Switzerland? We got you!
Here are three recommendations that tourists love.

1. Le Cerf

Le Cerf is a popular restuarant located in Rougemont. They offer a wide variety of traditional dishes made from ingredients around the region. Some notable dishes they have are fresh strips of veal from the Pays d'Enhaut, golden rosti and greed salad, malakoffs, and of course classic Swiss fondue! If you enjoy a cozy, rustic, cabin vibe with great mountain views in the distance, Le Cerf Rougemont is the perfect spot for you to dine at.

Reserve

2. Haus Hiltl

Looking for a restaurant that has vegetarian and vegan cuisine? Haus Hiltl is the place for you! Established in 1898, Haus Hiltl has held strong onto their company values of sustainability and responsibility for people, animals, and nature. They offer a variety of breakfast, lunch, and dinner options, as well as catering options for any big events. Their long-standing reputation makes them more than just a restaurant worth trying, but also an academy for cooking classes and holding events.

Reserve

3. Kronenhalle

Kronenhalle is one of the most iconic restaurants in Switzerland, noted for its historical roots and even considered a cultural landmark. If you enjoy literature, arts, and music, Kronenhalle is the hub where people alike gather for meal and community. Their cuisine is noted to be high-quality with attention to detail and care within its creation process. So if you're wanting to visit the finest dining in Switzerland, Kronenhalle is where you should head to!
