Great Beauty Awaits You

Switzerland is a destination rich with beautiful landscapes wherever you go. The Swiss Alps cover more than half of the country's terrains, allowing you to explore many activities along its slopes throughout the changing seasons. Besides the majestic mountains, explore the rich culture and traditions, along with learning more about the famous Swiss cheese, chocolate, and landmarks!

Travel Highlights

Curious on what the best spots around Switzerland are? Hover over these top highlighted destinations and discover which one best fits your interests!

Valle
Verzasca

A valley located in Locarno district. Verzasca is the perfect spot for hikers, mountain bikers, swimmers, and families. With so many activities to choose from, a beautiful river and scenery will accompany your day.

Bernese Oberlands

Bernese Oberlands is an iconic spot that is surrounded by high peaks and grassy green fields. Catch a scenic train ride through this area!

Learn More >>

Lauterbrunnen

Lauterbrunnen is a gorgeous town that embodies your expectations of Switzerland all in one place. Named after its 72 waterfalls, you can enjoy the views with wonderful hiking spots and a stroll through the village.

Learn More >>

Sachseln

Sachseln is your destination if you seek to experience the quiet, authentic, local charm of a Swiss village. Here you'll be surrounded by the views of the mountains, and there's a beautiful lake for activities or chilling.

Fun Facts

Four Official
Languages

Switzerland has four official languages: German, French, Italian, and Romansh.

Deepest Rail Tunnel

The deepest rail tunnel is located in Switzerland, and it's called the Gotthard Base Tunnel. It's 57.1 kilometers long!

Producer of more
than 500 cheeses

Each year, Switzerland produces more than 500 types of cheeses. All are made from Swiss milk and can contain different qualities: hard cheese, soft cheese, cream cheese, and more!
