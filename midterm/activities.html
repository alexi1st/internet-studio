Things To Do

Looking for what you could do during your visit to Switzerland?
Here is a list of three fun activities you can do!

1. Take a Scenic Hike

One thing you can do is go on a scenic hike. Switzerland is known for its gorgeous landscapes consisting of tall peaks and glistening bodies of water. Don't miss out on the opportunity to enjoy nature and see the views with your own eyes! The trail pictured above is one of the most popular trails yet called the Hardergrat Trail. However, there's so many to choose from, whether you want to go on a gentle trail, difficult trail, busy trail, or a less travelled trail. The network of hiking trails spans over 65,000km long, each with a valuable view worth seeing.

Book

2. Visit Iconic Castles

There are a multitude of notable castles throughout Switzerland that you can visit. These many castles symbolize and reflect on the history of Switzerland's strength and resilience. Whether you want to fulfill your fairytale dreams or admire the architecture of the past, there's many options to consider. Did you know that Prince Eric's castle from The Little Mermaid was inspired by the Château de Chillon (or Chillon Castle) located on the shores of Lake Geneva?

Book

3. Bungee Jumping

For anybody looking to experience an exciting boost of adrenaline, bungee jumping is an adventure you don't want to miss. You can know what's it's like to jump from the high Swiss landscapes sculpted by mother nature, or even recreate the famous James Bond jump bungee jump from GoldenEye located at the Verzasca Dam. Overall, it's a fun way to see the scenery Switzerland has to offer but with the twist of a thrilling experience.
