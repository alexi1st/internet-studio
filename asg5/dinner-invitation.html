Olivia Rodrigo is a very popular music artists of the new generation. I would like to have dinner with Olivia Rodrigo because I like her music but also because she's a Filipina queen. I often refer to her as "Ate Olivia" ('ate' is a respectful term to refer 'older sister' in Filipino) as a joke so I think it would be funny to actually meet her. Beyond just her music, I also think she's really nice and I look up to the fact that she isn't afraid to speak up for what she believes in while being a big artist. Overall, she seems like a very passionate and genuine person, so I feel like she would be a very pleasant person to meet and have a meal with.
