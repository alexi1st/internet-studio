Domain Discussion

I discussed the possible domain names with my peer. For the domain name "studysprout.co", the person I was discussing with really liked this one compared the others because it felt easy to say. Although I also liked this name, I mentioned how it felt a bit too specific with the "study" part in the name when my website is not exclusive to study spots but also work spots. As for "introspotive.com", at first she didn't like this one because it sounded like it was trying to hard. But since this was my favorite one out of the domain names I came up with, I tried to further justify/reason it with the meaning behind it and how it sounded cool, and eventually she was more convinced of it and it having a chance of being chosen. As for "spotbloom.co", she said it was kinda forgettable and made no sense, which I kinda agreed with as I felt pretty indifferent towards this name. I also shared some of my visions for the aesthetics/style regarding each domain name, since each one gives off a certain vibe. It was agreed upon that "introspotive.com" had more of a modern, minimalistic vibe compared to the other two, where I thought they had a more earthy/growth aspect to them.

The domain name that I ultimately like the best is "introspotive.com". I particularly like this one because it's a play on the word "introspective", which is kinda catchy, creative, and interesting. I think it reflects on the concept of one being able to find what their preferences are whenever they go to an environment with the means of being in a state of focus while doing work or studying.
