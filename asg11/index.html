Gihun

The second pick for my team is Gihun from the Squid Games. I chose Gihun because not only is he a strategizer, but he also has intense main character energy and therefore by law he just cannot lose. Harold

Harold is my third pick for my team. I picked Harold because he has already proven himself in dodgeball through a Total Drama episode. He is able to dodge the balls with ease which will frustrate opponents and diminish their energy throughout the duration of the game. While dodging is his strong suite, this valuable skill is what could ultimately take our team to the end and possibly leave him as the last one standing.

Chang

Chang is my fourth pick. Chang, while probably not that good at dodgeball, I feel would provide the team with an energy that is unmatched. A motivating but simultaneously degrading team spirit that is less than ideal but somewhat necessary. This energy will be the main catalyst leading up to Jeff's big speech which will turn the tide. If not, Chang is also known for his rather unethical practices regarding getting what he wants, so the win will be earned no matter what through his (unjust) efforts.

Chill Guy

Chill guy is my last pick. I chose him because I recognize that there is always that one player in each team who lowkey does not contribute anything but is there for moral support and to spread good vibes. So Chill Guy is here because he'll turn the inevitable tension of the team to a more serene, laid-back vibe with just his presence. He'll keep everybody grounded with reality and serve as a reminder not to blow up any emotions too intensely. He's the overarching peacemaker.
